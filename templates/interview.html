Interview Preparation Guide

Get ready to shine in your next interview

🗣️ Common Interview Questions

  • Tell me about yourself.
  • Why do you want to work here?
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Describe a challenge you faced and how you handled it.
  • Why should we hire you?
  • Tell me about a time you worked in a team.

🌟 STAR Technique (for Behavioral Questions)

S – Situation: Describe the background or challenge.

T – Task: What was your responsibility?

A – Action: What steps did you take?

R – Result: What was the outcome?

💡 Tip: Practice storytelling using STAR to make your answers clear and structured.

✅ Pre-Interview Checklist

  • Research the company and its mission
  • Read the job description thoroughly
  • Prepare 3–5 strong questions to ask
  • Dress appropriately and arrive early
  • Bring extra copies of your resume
  • Review your achievements and portfolio
  • Practice mock interviews with a friend

⚠️ Do’s & Don’ts

✅ Do's

  • Maintain eye contact
  • Be confident, but humble
  • Give examples when answering
  • Be honest about your experiences

🚫 Don’ts

  • Interrupt the interviewer
  • Speak negatively about past employers
  • Overshare personal details
  • Memorize answers word-for-word
